Welcome to Our Studio

Welcome to ROURKELA MEHNDI & TATTOO JAIPURI STYLE, where traditional artistry meets modern innovation. Located in the heart of Rourkela at Forum Galleria Mall, we specialize in creating breathtaking Mehndi designs and stunning tattoo artworks.

Our expert artists bring years of experience in traditional Jaipuri and Rajasthani Mehndi patterns, as well as contemporary tattoo designs. Whether you're preparing for your wedding day or looking for the perfect custom tattoo, we're here to make your vision a reality.
